Work

My electrical engineering experiences lie primarily in the oil and gas industry where I have worked for Precision Drilling as a Field Automation Engineer-in-Training (EIT) for three years and then as an Electrical EIT since the start of 2022. In these roles, I have been responsible for the entire life cycle of technical projects meant to modernize industrial drilling equipment. This includes the initial prototyping, feasibility and cost studies, to the on-site commissioning and continued emergency support of our products. Due to the flexible nature of my team, my work has varied from designing simple electrical and programmable logic controller (PLC) systems, to high-tech Lidar systems and big data initiatives.

Personal project experiences include Printed Circuit Board (PCB) and electrical system design for the University of Alberta’s SPEAR robotics club as well as taking a mixed-signal hardware design course in my spare time. I have been working on improving my programming through online courses, textbooks, and side projects (e.g., this website) and am proficient with Python and have some JavaScript/C experience.
